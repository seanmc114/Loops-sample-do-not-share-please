Making a game — the short version

Just 4 easy steps

What this does

You give it a topic — paste in some notes, or just type what you want (like "Irish verbs" or "our new starter checklist") — and it turns that into a game with questions to practise.

How you move through it

  • 1Pick a category and give your game a name
  • 2Tell it what the game should be about
  • 3Play it yourself first, to make sure it's right
  • 4Publish it so others can play too

Tip

Big blue buttons move you to the next step. You can also tap the words along the top (Basics, Content, Preview, Publish) to jump around.
